Introducing Catalog

March 5, 2022

I'm excited to announce that I just released Catalog.

The story behind this site

Catalog started from my frustration of finding an entryway bench that I liked and suited my style. I kept looking at the same website repeatedly, thinking it could not be the only option, so I did what every engineer would do! So I built something to solve it and created a website that allows you to discover the best products and designs of furniture which match your taste and budget.

Furniture are the soul of your house.

The right piece of furniture can make you feel at home, but finding the perfect one is hard. It defines how you live and feels at home, so choosing wisely is important. The right pieces will help you create an ambiance that reflects your style and personality while providing functional benefits such as storage and organization. There are so many options out there, but it's so challenging to discover them.

You spend a lot of time in your house, so you should like the furniture. You have to consider many things when finding furniture: price, look, and size, among others. The best way to choose what kind of furniture is by using Catalog.

They are more, better, and cheaper furniture options that you believe

The best thing about Catalog is that it will make you discover stores you didn't know about. It can help you find better quality furniture, better looking, based on your budget and lifestyle.

Catalog aggregate 100+ furniture websites to make finding the ideal furniture for you easier.
